About Moremakers Advisory

Moremakers Advisory is a research and analytics division of Moremakers Investment CC, focused on delivering accurate, evidence-based market research, economic insights, and business intelligence across Namibia and Southern Africa.

We help decision-makers in both the private and public sectors make smarter choices through data-driven insights that inform strategy, policy, and performance.

Sectors We Serve:

  • Retail
  • Energy & Utilities
  • Healthcare
  • Telecommunications
  • Automotive
  • Financial Sector
  • Public Sector

Research Area

  • Market Research – Analyze consumer behavior, pricing trends, and competitor activity.
  • Political & Public Opinion Research – Capture views on leadership and governance.
  • Policy Research – Evaluate the effectiveness of public policies.
  • Economic Research – Study economic trends and resource allocation.
  • Healthcare Research – Assess patient satisfaction and service delivery.
  • Social Policy Research – Evaluate government and community program impacts.
  • Environmental & Climate Policy Research – Explore climate and sustainability policies.
  • Technology & Innovation Policy Research – Analyze technology’s role in development.

Flagship Products

  • Customer Satisfaction Index – National and sectoral customer satisfaction rankings.
  • Voice of the Customer (VoC) – Real-time feedback tools for service improvement.
  • Customer Service Benchmarking Index – Compare service performance regionally.
  • Namibian Executive Outlook Survey (NEOS) – Insights from business leaders on the economy.

Our Core Services

We provide specialized research services designed to help companies and institutions navigate markets, understand consumers, monitor competitors and evaluate policies.

  • Price Surveys – Monitor product prices across markets and competitors.
  • Product Availability Checks – Identify stock shortages and supply gaps.
  • Competitor Price Monitoring – Track and analyze competitors’ pricing strategies.
  • Retail Insights – Collect in-store data on products and trends.
  • Market Entry Research – Provide intelligence to support expansion into new markets.
  • Custom Research – Design research tailored to your business needs.

Why Choose Us?

  • Deep understanding of local markets.
  • Network of field researchers across Namibia.
  • Experience across multiple sectors.
  • Focus on accuracy, relevance, and impact.
  • We deliver not just data, but actionable intelligence.
